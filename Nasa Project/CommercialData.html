Commercial Data

The project goal is to establish a DEAP institute and partnership in order to increase the number and research capacity as well as prepare a future workforce for data-intensive space-based earth sciences at HBCUs. Commercial data refers to data created and provided by commercial entities rather than government agencies. NASA’s Commercial Smallsat Data Acquisition (CSDA) program identifies, evaluates, and acquires remote sensing imagery and data from commercial sources that support NASA’s Earth science research and application activities.
